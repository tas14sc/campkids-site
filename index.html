The EQ Treasure Map

Four Islands. One Journey.

Every Camp Kid sets out across four islands — each one a new frontier of emotional intelligence, guided by characters your kids will never forget.

I
Redfish Island
Self-Awareness

Campers begin by discovering who they truly are — their strengths, their feelings, and the unique gifts they bring to the world. Every great journey starts here.

II
Evergreen Island
Social Awareness

Through empathy and community, campers learn to see the world through others' eyes — understanding how their choices ripple out to the people around them.

III
Coral Cove Island
Self-Management

Canoe Kathy and Ranger Ross guide campers through decision-making, emotional regulation, and the power of thinking before acting. Mindful choices lead to big wins.

IV
Deep Blue Island
Social Skills

The final destination — collaboration, negotiation, and the truth every Camp Kid discovers: TOGETHER is always better than alone.

The Heart Behind Camp Kids

Born in a Classroom,
Built for a Lifetime

Camp Kids grew out of a real classroom in Franklin, Tennessee, where educator and artist Debby Moore transformed her space into a full campsite — complete with characters, a treasure map, and an adventure that made young learners owners of their own learning.

What started as a creative teaching environment became something far bigger: a complete framework for social and emotional learning that moves young minds from literal to figurative thinking, from self-focused to community-minded, from reactive to emotionally equipped.

Every character Debby drew, every island she named, every workbook she designed carries the same conviction — that if you can captivate a child's soul, everything meaningful will stick and stay.
